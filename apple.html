Apple

Requirements for Apple

NPK Ratio - 12:12:12 or 12:15:15
Soil Type - Well-drained, Loam Soil (Soil having Depth of 45 cm)
pH of Soil - 5.5 to 6.5
Climate - 21-240C
Duration of Fruit - 130 to 150 Days
Production Duration of Tree - Between 4 to 8 years
Rainfall - 1000 mm to 1250 mm
        NPK Ratio  - Nitrogen, Phosphorus and Potassium Ratio
        pH of Soil - To measure the Acidic or Basicity of a Soil
