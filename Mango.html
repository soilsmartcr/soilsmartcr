Mango

Requirements for Mango

NPK Ratio - 10:20:20
Soil Type - Lateritic, Alluvial, Sandy Loam and Sandy Soil (Ideal - Red Loamy Soils)
pH of Soil - 5.5 to 7.5
Climate - Humid and Dry Conditions
Duration of Fruit - 12 to 15 Weeks
Production Duration of Tree - 40 years or more
Rainfall - 890 mm to 1015 mm in a year
        NPK Ratio  - Nitrogen, Phosphorus and Potassium Ratio
        pH of Soil - To measure the Acidic or Basicity of a Soil
