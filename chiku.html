Sapodilla

Requirements for Sapodilla (Chiku)

NPK Ratio - 12:12:12 or 12:15:15
Soil Type - Shallow Clay Soil
pH of Soil - 6.0 to 8.0
Climate - Warm and Humid Climate
Temperature - 10-380C
Duration of Fruit - Twice in year
Production Duration of Tree - 7 to 15 years
Rainfall - 1250 mm to 2500 mm 
        NPK Ratio  - Nitrogen, Phosphorus and Potassium Ratio
        pH of Soil - To measure the Acidic or Basicity of a Soil
