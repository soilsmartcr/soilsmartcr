Sun Flower

Requirements for Sun Flower

NPK Ratio - 60:80:60
Soil Type - Sandy Loam Soil (Well-drained)
pH of Soil - 6.0 to 6.8
Climate - Cool Climate for Seeding Stage & Warn Climate for Flowering Stage
Temperature - 20-300C
Duration - 90 to 100 Days
Rainfall - 300mm to 350mm 
        NPK Ratio  - Nitrogen, Phosphorus and Potassium Ratio
        pH of Soil - To measure the Acidic or Basicity of a Soil
